SiteSort – Logo Concepts

5 design directions. Pick your favourite — or mix elements from several.

Option 1 — Site First

Bold two-line wordmark paired with an amber hard hat. Instantly communicates construction. Strong at all sizes.

Option 2 — Sort Lines

Descending sort bars on the left double as a "signal strength" icon. Clean, tech-forward, memorable. Works well in blue.

Option 3 — On-Site

Location pin icon with a city-skyline inside. Green signals "go / on track". Works well for a compliance-focused brand.

Option 4 — Monogram

Amber "SS" badge on dark background. Premium and authoritative — works well on dark nav bars, hard hats, or hi-vis vests.

Option 5 — Blueprint

Blueprint grid with yellow building outlines on a deep blue square. Suggests precision, planning, and professionalism.
